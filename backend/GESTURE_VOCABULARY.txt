#             NEXUS GLOVE - FULL GESTURE VOCABULARY (40 WORDS)                #
###############################################################################

PART 1: THE ESSENTIALS (1-20)
Focus: Survival, basic needs, and immediate response.
01. OPEN          - Neutral State: Hand flat, fingers straight and spread. (Baseline)
02. YES           - Fist Nod: Make a fist and tilt the wrist up and down.
03. NO            - Snap: Index and middle fingers snap down to touch the thumb.
04. HELLO         - Salute: Flat hand starts at the temple and moves out to the side.
05. THANK YOU     - Chin Out: Touch chin with flat hand, move it forward and down.
06. PLEASE        - Chest Circle: Flat hand held upright, moving in a circular motion.
07. HELP          - Thumbs Up: Make a fist with the thumb up, move the hand upwards.
08. WATER         - "W" to Chin: Index, middle, ring fingers up; tap index to chin.
09. FOOD          - Tap Mouth: Fingers all touching thumb (O-shape), tap your lips.
10. TOILET        - "T" Shake: Thumb tucked between index/middle finger, shake fist.
11. SICK          - Touch Head: Middle finger bent forward, touch your forehead.
12. PAIN          - Twist: Point index fingers toward each other and "twist" hands.
13. STOP          - Flat Down: Flat hand moves down sharply like a chop.
14. GO            - Point: Index finger points and hand moves away from body.
15. ME            - Point Self: Index finger points directly to your chest.
16. YOU           - Point Forward: Index finger points directly at the person.
17. GOOD          - Thumb Up: Standard "Thumbs up" held steady.
18. BAD           - Pinky Out: Make a fist but keep the pinky finger pointed up.
19. WANT          - Claw Pull: Fingers slightly bent, palms up, pulling toward self.
20. MORE          - Tap Together: Bring all fingertips together and tap them.


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PART 2: EXPANSION (21-40)
Focus: Emotions, people, and daily life context.

21. MOTHER        - Chin Tap: Open hand, thumb taps the chin.
22. FATHER        - Head Tap: Open hand, thumb taps the forehead.
23. BROTHER       - Hat Pull: Thumb and index "pull" imaginary cap brim at forehead.
24. SISTER        - Chin Pull: Same as brother, but at the chin.
25. HOME          - Cheek Tap: Fingers together, tap chin then tap upper cheek.
26. SCHOOL        - Clap: Flat hand "claps" against the palm (or held flat).
27. WORK          - Hammer: Two fists tapping each other (or fist tapping palm).
28. TIME          - Wrist Tap: Index finger taps the top of the wrist (watch area).
29. TODAY         - Now Down: "Y" hand shape (thumb/pinky out) moving down twice.
30. NOW           - Single Drop: "Y" hand shape drops down once firmly.
31. HAPPY         - Chest Swipe: Flat hand brushes upward against chest twice.
32. SAD           - Face Drop: Fingers spread in front of face, dropping down slowly.
33. ANGRY         - Claw Face: Claw hand pulls upward from stomach toward face.
34. SCARED        - Chest Block: Both hands (or one) open wide in front of chest.
35. HOT           - Breath Blow: Hand in "C" shape near mouth, rotate outward.
36. COLD          - Shiver: Fists closed, arms/hands shaking near the chest.
37. BIG           - Space Out: Hands move far apart from each other.
38. SMALL         - Pinch: Thumb and index finger held close together.
39. LIKE          - Heart Pull: Middle finger and thumb "pinch" and pull from chest.
40. SORRY         - Fist Circle: Closed fist moving in a circle over the heart.
